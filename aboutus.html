About US

Our Story

Well Being Center began as a family therapy institution in the year 2013. As our client relationships flourished, we came to a realization, that life is broad, and encompasses a lot of factors that affect the decisions and choices people make. In 2019, we decided to expand our institution, and began offering career guidance, behavioral modification, marriage counseling, financial management advice general guidance in coping with life's challenges.

Our Goal

To provide goal-oriented guidance particularly to assist in making decisions that align with your life goals,to understand yourself and make a difference in your family and the community. To provide programmes that combine one-to-one interventions focused on building individual resilience, with family-based interventions focused on building trust, communication,and functioning in an all-round manner
