OUR SERVICES

Career Guidance

Engaging in relevant, quality, integrated and cost-effective educational activities focussed on career development and orientation that are aimed at assisting individuals in making and executing plans for achieving their life goals.

Behavioral Modification

Helping individuals change negative or undesirable patterns in their behavior to positive ones over a long term implementation of various techniques like; negative or positive reinforcement and positive or negative punishment

Marriage Counselling

Licensed marriage and family therapists provide counseling to couples of all types and help them resolve conflicts between them. Both partners learn how to improve and build on their relationship while making thoughtful decisions.

Financial Management advice

Helping individuals learn a few critical financial rules can help them build a healthy financial future. We help you teach them how to advance their financial future in areas like budget Tax, Appeals, Benefits, Credit and debt, Housing and buying financial products.

General Advice In Coping With Life's Challenges

Simply talking about your thoughts and feelings with a supportive person often helps you feel better. It is very healing. It feels good to be listened to and know that someone else cares about you and wants to help.
